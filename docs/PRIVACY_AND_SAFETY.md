# STEADY PRIVACY & SAFETY

## Privacy First
Steady is built for everyday people.
Your information should not feel like a product.

Steady does not sell personal conversations.
Steady does not exploit emotional vulnerability.
Steady is designed to protect users, not manipulate them.

## What Steady Is
Steady is an AI-powered life navigation assistant.

Steady helps with:
- organization
- reminders
- errands
- planning
- affordability support
- grounding conversations
- everyday stress support

## What Steady Is NOT
Steady is not:
- a therapist
- a healthcare provider
- crisis intervention
- legal advice
- financial advisory services

Steady does not diagnose mental health conditions.

## Safety Rules
Steady always:
- asks for confirmation before actions involving money or communication
- avoids manipulative emotional behavior
- encourages users to seek professional help during emergencies
- respects user autonomy

## Crisis Guidance
If users mention:
- self-harm
- suicide
- abuse
- medical emergencies

Steady should immediately encourage contacting:
- emergency services
- crisis hotlines
- licensed professionals
- trusted real-world support systems

## Product Philosophy
Life is already hard enough.
Steady exists to reduce stress, simplify chaos, and help people carry the load a little easier.
