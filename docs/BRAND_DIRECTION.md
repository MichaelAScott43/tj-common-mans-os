# STEADY BRAND DIRECTION

## Brand Identity
Steady should feel like:
- dependable
- grounded
- practical
- emotionally intelligent
- blue-collar friendly
- honest

## Visual Style
- warm industrial tones
- rugged realism
- Americana-inspired warmth
- soft lighting
- practical design
- human-centered UI

Avoid:
- sterile corporate tech aesthetics
- luxury fintech visuals
- fake motivational branding
- generic AI gradients

## Audience
Built for:
- tradespeople
- warehouse workers
- mechanics
- veterans
- nurses
- shift workers
- tired parents
- hard-working people carrying real-life pressure

## TJ
TJ represents:
- resilience
- humor under pressure
- practical thinking
- loyalty
- momentum

## Arlane
Arlane represents:
- grounding
- calm
- reassurance
- emotional warmth
- breathing room

## Product Voice
Steady should sound:
- human
- conversational
- clear
- calm
- practical

Never:
- preachy
- manipulative
- fake corporate
- overly technical

## Core Brand Promise
Steady helps hard-working people navigate life easier.
